About

Radwan Altaleb

  • 1980s

    Humble Beginnings

    Radwan altaleb was born in Damascus, Syria, in 1974 He started to learn Oud from his father and his oldest sister at age of 8. His passion is for the old oud sound, especially instruments made in Syria.

  • 1990s

    Academic Studies

    Radwan went on to join the Syrian middle institute of music at the age 12 and then joined the high institute of music in 1992. Radwan graduated in 1997 in Damascus, Syria with a Degree in music performance mastering Oud.

  • 2000s

    Professional Career

    Radwan went to join National Syrian Orchestra, Palmyra Band as an Oudist.

  • 2010s

    The Move to Canada

    Radwan eventually moved to Canada after the war in Syria and founded Syca Band which Became the the Arabest Orchestra later in 2016. Radwan now teaches Oud and lives in Ontario, Canada.
